## The Prompt

```
Generate the text "Hello world" with world in yellow highlighting. Black text, white background. That words should be shown next to each other in the center of the screen.

Animation: Let the words fade in from left to right and then highlight "world" yellow. Mimic a typewriter effect for the text appearance with a blinking cursor at the end of the text.
```

## What This Generates

This prompt creates a classic typewriter animation effect with:

- Text that appears character by character from left to right
- A blinking cursor at the end of the text during typing
- Yellow highlighting applied to specific words after typing completes
- Clean black text on white background
- Centered positioning on screen

## Key Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Typewriter Effect">

  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Blinking Cursor">
    A cursor blinks at the end of the text while typing, adding authenticity to the typewriter effect and making it feel more dynamic.

## Customization Tips

### Change the Text
Replace `"Hello world"` with your own message. You can make it as short or long as you need.

### Adjust Colors
- Change `yellow highlighting` to any color (e.g., "red highlighting", "blue highlighting")
- Modify `Black text, white background` to match your brand colors

### Control the Speed
Add timing instructions like:
- "Type slowly" or "Type quickly"
- "Pause for 2 seconds before highlighting"

### Highlight Different Words
Specify which words should be highlighted:
- "Highlight the first word in blue"
- "Highlight all capital words in red"
